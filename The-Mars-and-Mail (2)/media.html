The Japanese Billionaire: Maezawa

Yusaku Maezawa has exploded on social media as the Japanese billionaire who wants to be the first passenger on the BFR. The BFR is scheduled to make a lap around the moon as a test in 2023 and Maezawa will be the first astronaut on the BFR as it makes this trip. He is known to be supplying SpaceX with an unknown amount of funding in exchange for being the first passenger. The billionaire who was known for creating Zozotown (logo shown), a japanese attire company is now wanting to get involved in the aerospace industry. This is where much of the social implications come into play. In the second week of January 2020, Maezawa has stated on social media that he is looking for a “special” women to take with him on the lap around Mars. He proceeded to say that he is taking applications. By just about three days later, Maezawa received over 20 000 applications. Although this is very unique, he has received much backlash for it, as much of society doesn't approve of these actions. It has been expressed over Twitter by many that his contest does not stay true to the many women who are working tirelessly to be astronauts. Many have said that they do not appreciate the idea of the first women to Mars would be chosen by a billionaire for a romantic relationship rather than through space programs.
